The use of the Doddly app is also governed by the Terms and Conditions. We invite you to read them carefully together with this privacy policy.

Doddly is an application designed to help you track your daily hydration through a virtual companion (Doddly, a water drop) that reacts to your progress. Doddly is built on the principle of data minimization: it does not require account creation and does not collect information that could personally identify you.

1. Data we do NOT collect

Doddly does not collect, request, or store:

  • Name, email, or other identifying data
  • Contact information
  • Location information
  • Health data transmitted to third parties
  • Financial data
  • Advertising or tracking identifiers

The app does not require account creation and does not link any information to your identity.

2. Your hydration data

All content you enter — water amounts logged, daily goal, preferred unit of measurement (ml/oz), your virtual companion's state and progress, reminder settings — is stored exclusively on your device. This data is never sent to us or to third parties. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes this data.

3. No external services

Unlike some of our other apps, Doddly works entirely offline: it does not contact any external service, has no remote servers, and uses no cloud caching or syncing of any kind. All app logic (hydration calculation, companion state, reminders) runs locally on your device.

4. Device permissions

The app may request the following permission:

  • Notifications: for hydration reminders, managed entirely locally on the device. No notification is sent via external (remote push) servers — all reminders are scheduled locally by the app itself.

You can revoke this permission at any time from your operating system's Settings, without this preventing use of the app's core features.

5. Third-party services

Doddly does not use analytics tools, advertising, user behavior tracking, or any third-party SDK or library for data collection.

6. Security

Since Doddly does not send data over the Internet and has no remote servers, the risk to your privacy is minimal: your data never leaves your device.

7. Your rights

In accordance with GDPR and similar regulations, you may at any time revoke permissions granted to the app from your operating system's Settings, and delete all your data by uninstalling the app.

8. Note on the nature of the app

Doddly is a tool designed to help you build the habit of drinking water regularly. Doddly does not give medical advice: it is solely a personal tracking indicator, not a medical device, and it does not replace the advice of a physician. Hydration goals suggested in the app are generic and indicative. If you have a condition that requires specific control of fluid intake (for example kidney or heart conditions), consult your doctor before following any goal set in the app.

9. Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated in the future, for example when new features are added. The most recent version will always be available at this address.
