Before following any fluid intake goal, particularly if you have a pre-existing medical condition (kidney disease, heart conditions, or other conditions requiring fluid control), consult your doctor.

5.1 How we calculate your suggested daily goal

If you choose to enter your weight, biological sex, age, and activity level during setup, Doddly uses that data to suggest a more personalized hydration goal. To do this, we took inspiration from the general guidance published by EFSA (the European Food Safety Authority) on water intake, and from ESPEN for the age-related adjustment. The exact coefficients we use in the calculation are our own design choice, inspired by these sources — they're not figures reproduced identically from any official body, so the suggested goal remains indicative, not a clinical value.

If you'd rather not enter this data, that's totally fine: Doddly will still suggest a reasonable default goal, which you can change anytime from Settings.
